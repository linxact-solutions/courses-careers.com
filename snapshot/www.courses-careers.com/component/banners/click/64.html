Master of Business Administration (MBA)

In this course students can study for an MBA that applies the teaching methods and concepts commonly used in North America and Europe, but with a unique focus on the Asia Pacific.

The APU-MBA offers case method education focused on businesses in the Asia Pacific region together with a strong foundation in established management techniques.

 

Students in the APU Master of Business Administration can choose to focus their studies in the following concentrations:

  • Finance Concentration
  • International Business and Marketing Concentration
  • Innovation and Technology Management Concentration
  • Comparative Institution Design for Transition Economy and Business Management

Degree awarded upon completion: Master of Business Administration (MBA)

Duration of degree program: Two years full time. (Completion in 1.5 years may be possible).

Enrollment periods: Early April and late September every year

*Spring Option: Please note that while there are two enrollment periods each year for the MBA program, the Required Subjects will be offered only in the Fall Semester.
